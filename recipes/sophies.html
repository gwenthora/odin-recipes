Sophie's Breakfast

A hot classic breakfast that is protein heavy to give plenty of energy for a day of chores.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Thick slices of bacon
  • Loaf of fresh bread
  • Hard cheese for side. Sliced or cubed.
  • Butter (if desired)

Preparation

  1. In a large pan lay out desired slices of bacon. Turn over when one side cooked.
  2. When second side is mostly cooked add cracked eggs to the other side of the pan.
  3. Serve when Eggs are cooked to preference with a large slice of bread and cheese on the side.

Enjoy!
