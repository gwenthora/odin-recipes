Chihiro's Steamed Buns

Fluffy and warm steamed buns, perfect for an on the go meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1 1/2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 2 Tbsp canola oil
  • 2 Cups of cake flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • Read bean paste and/or cooked shredded pork

Preparation

  1. Dissolve sugar in warm water and add yeast. Lit sit covered for several minutes
  2. Whisk dry ingredients and make a well in center
  3. Whisk canola oil into yeast until combined
  4. Pour mixture into well of dry ingredients and combine with yoru hands. Knead the dough until smooth
  5. Form dough into a ball and place in lightly greased bowl with a damp towel over the top. Wait one our for rise
  6. Sprinkle a small amount of flour on a clean, falt work surface and roll dough into a log shape
  7. Cut dough into sections
  8. Roll each dough piece into a ball and cover with damp cloth and let rest for 15 minutes
  9. Prepare steamer
  10. Using rolling pin, flatten each ball, pleace spoon of desired filling into center, and gently gather sides forming pleats and presssing together. Close bun by pinching dough together and twisting
  11. Steam for 15 minutes

Enjoy!
