Ponyo's Ramen

Steamy ramen noodles with chopped green onions and protein.

Ingredients

  • Thick sliced ham
  • Fresh green onion stalks
  • Eggs
  • Ramen noodles

Preparation

  1. Boil eggs and peel shell when done
  2. Chop green onions into thin slices
  3. Cut thick slices of ham to fit in bowl
  4. In a bowl layer dry ramen noodles and prepared ingredients on top.
  5. Add boiling water to bowl covering all ingredients and cover for 3-5 minutes.

Enjoy!
